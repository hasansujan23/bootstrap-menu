Udemy

Udemy.com is an online learning platform. It is aimed at professional adults.[2] Unlike academic MOOC programs driven by traditional collegiate coursework, Udemy uses content from online content creators to sell for profit.[3] Udemy provides tools which enable users to create a course, promote it and earn money from student tuition charges.
